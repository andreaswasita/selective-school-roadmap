### This is the Year Reading "Clicks"

### Building Comprehension Skills

Teach these 7 comprehension strategies explicitly:

| Strategy | How to Teach |
|----------|-------------|
| **Predicting** | "What do you think will happen next? Why?" |
| **Questioning** | "What questions do you have about this chapter?" |
| **Clarifying** | "Is there a word you don't understand? Let's look it up." |
| **Summarising** | "Tell me what happened in 2-3 sentences." |
| **Connecting** | "Does this remind you of anything in your life?" |
| **Visualising** | "What did you picture in your mind when you read that?" |
| **Inferring** | "The text doesn't say it directly, but what do you think the character is feeling?" |

## ðŸ”¢ Mathematics â€” Times Tables Focus

### Why Times Tables Matter So Much

Times tables are the **foundation for everything** that comes next: division, fractions, algebra, and the selective test. Children who have automatic times table recall perform significantly better in maths.

### Attack Plan

| Term | Tables to Master | Method |
|------|-----------------|--------|
| Term 1 | 2x, 10x | Skip counting songs, patterns |
| Term 2 | 5x, 4x | Building on 2x (double the doubles) |
| Term 3 | 3x, 6x | Songs, rapid recall drills |
| Term 4 | 7x, 8x, 9x | 9x finger trick, practice |

### Proven Methods for Times Tables

1. **Songs & Chants** â€” Multiplication rap songs (YouTube: "Times Tables Rock Stars")
2. **Skip counting** â€” Count by 3s, 4s, etc. while bouncing a ball
3. **Visual arrays** â€” Draw dots in rows to show 3 Ã— 4 = 12
4. **Speed drills** â€” Timed worksheets (2 minutes, get faster each week)
5. **Games** â€” Card games, dice games, apps
6. **Real life** â€” "How many wheels on 4 cars?" (4 Ã— 4)
