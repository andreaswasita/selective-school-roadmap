## About This Guide

**Current situation:** 5 years old, recognises letters but cannot yet read independently.

**Goal:** Build a strong academic foundation to:
1. Enter an **Opportunity Class (OC)** in Year 5 (test taken in Year 4, age ~9)
2. Enter a **Selective High School** in Year 7 (test taken in Year 6, age ~11)
3. Develop a lifelong love of learning in **STEM, medicine, or technology**

**Philosophy:** This guide follows proven educational research â€” nurturing intrinsic motivation, building strong foundations before speed, and making learning enjoyable. No child should be burned out before they reach high school.
